Where at?

The common room is situated at the 11th floor of the building and includes a terrasse with an amazing view of Aarhus city and the harbour.

What for?

This is a meeting place for all the residents. The room is equipped with:
• larger kitchen area
• big TV
• billiard table
• table football
• many other board games etc.
You can book some parts of the room for a private event in the calendar section.

Where at?

The study room is situated on the ground floor of the building.

What for?

This is a quiet space for studying for all the residents.
It is equipped with printers, study desks, computers and a free wi-fi. Beware that thee room is not suitable for large groups of people.

Where at?

You can find the laundry room in the basement. Don’t forget to book the machine before using it. Please notice the option to book the drier.

What for?

You will find vacuum cleaners in the launderette. You are very welcome to use them. However, remember to return them after use!

Where at?

The cycling room is in the basement, available for all the residents.

What for?

You will find an air pump as well as some repairment instruments. Its free for all residents.
